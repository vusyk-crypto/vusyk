Start Investing

To invest in the VUSYK coin, you need to follow a few simple steps. Below is a detailed instruction:

Step 1: Creating a Cryptocurrency Wallet

To work with the VUSYK token, which is developed on the Solana blockchain, you need to register a wallet. We recommend using the Phantom wallet or the official Solana wallet.

Registering a Phantom wallet:

  • Visit the Phantom website.
  • Download and install the extension for your browser.
  • Open the extension and follow the instructions to create a new wallet. Write down your recovery phrase and keep it in a safe place.

Registering a Solana wallet:

  • Visit the official Solana website.
  • Download and install the official wallet for your device.
  • Create a new wallet, write down your recovery phrase, and keep it in a safe place.

Step 2: Buying the VUSYK Token

Funding your wallet:

After creating the wallet, fund it with SOL tokens (the native cryptocurrency of Solana). You can buy SOL on exchanges such as Binance or any other cryptocurrency exchange that supports Solana.

Connecting to a decentralized exchange:

Visit a decentralized exchange that supports Solana tokens. In our case, it's Raydium. Connect your Phantom or Solana wallet to the exchange using the appropriate option on the website.

Buying the VUSYK token:

After connecting the wallet, find the VUSYK/SOL pair on the exchange. Enter the amount of SOL you want to exchange for VUSYK and confirm the transaction.

Step 3: Checking Transactions

Checking transactions in Solscan:

After completing the transaction, you can check its status on Solscan. Visit the Solscan website. Enter your wallet address in the search bar to view all transactions related to your wallet.

By following this instruction, you can easily create a wallet, buy the VUSYK token, and check your transactions. If you have any further questions, please contact our support service.
