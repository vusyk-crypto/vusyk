Start Investing

To invest in the VUSYK coin, you need to follow a few simple steps. Below is a detailed instruction:

Step 1: Creating a Cryptocurrency Wallet

To work with the VUSYK token, which is developed on the Solana blockchain, you need to register a wallet. We recommend using the Phantom wallet or the official Solana wallet.

Registering a Phantom Wallet:
  • Visit the Phantom website.
  • Download and install the extension for your browser.
  • Open the extension and follow the instructions to create a new wallet. Write down your recovery phrase and keep it in a safe place.

Step 2: Funding Your Wallet with SOL

Obtaining Your Wallet Address:

  • Open the Phantom wallet and copy your wallet address.

Buying SOL on an Exchange:

  • Go to any cryptocurrency exchange (for example, Binance).
  • Register on the exchange if you do not have an account yet.
  • Buy SOL using your credit card or another payment method.

Transferring SOL to the Phantom Wallet:

  • Withdraw the purchased SOL to your Phantom wallet address.
  • Go to the "Withdraw" section on the exchange, enter the Phantom wallet address, and the amount of SOL you want to transfer.

Step 3: Using Raydium to Buy VUSYK

Connecting the Wallet to Raydium:

  • Go to the Raydium website.
  • Click on "Connect Wallet" and select the Phantom wallet.
  • Confirm the connection in the Phantom window.

Finding the VUSYK Token:

  • On the Raydium homepage, select the "Swap" section.
  • In the "To" field, enter "VUS". If the token does not appear, copy and paste the contract address:
    Sp88sMW4Mf1836UXx8b1vw96bz4VQo5SaVMc2svzbDF

    Hover over the token and click "Add token".

The token icon has exclusively this image:

If you see a different image, then this token has no relation to the project!

Entering the Amount of SOL:

  • In the "From" field, enter the amount of SOL you want to exchange for VUSYK.
  • You will see how much VUSYK you will receive for the specified amount of SOL.

Confirming the Exchange:

  • Click the "Swap" button to confirm the exchange.
  • Confirm the transaction in the Phantom window.
  • Wait for the transaction to complete.

Step 4: Adding VUSYK to Your Wallet

  • In the Phantom wallet window, click on the "+" icon next to "Your Assets".
  • Enter the contract address of the VUSYK token (you can find it on the project website or in directories).
  • Add the token to your list of assets.

Step 5: Saving and Managing Your Investment

Checking Balance:

  • Open the Phantom wallet and ensure that VUSYK tokens are displayed in your account.

Market Monitoring:

  • Track the market price of VUSYK on Raydium or other exchanges to make informed investment decisions.

Selling Tokens (if needed):

  • To sell VUSYK, follow the same steps as for buying but swap SOL and VUSYK in the "Swap" section.

Additional Tips

  • Security: Always keep your seed phrase in a safe place and do not share it with anyone.
  • Fees: Make sure you have enough SOL to cover transaction fees.
  • Research: Conduct your own research on the VUSYK project before investing.

Checking Transactions

Checking Transactions on Solscan:

  • After completing the transaction, you can check its status on Solscan
  • Enter your wallet address in the search bar to view all transactions associated with your wallet.

By following this instruction, you can easily create a wallet, buy the VUSYK token, and check your transactions. If you have any additional questions, please contact our support service.
